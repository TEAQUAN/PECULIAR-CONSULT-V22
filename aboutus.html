About Us

Welcome to Peculiar Consulting - Your Trusted Accounting Partner

At Peculiar Consulting, we take pride in providing accurate and reliable accounting services to individuals, small businesses, and large organizations. Our dedicated team of experts is committed to delivering excellent results and building long-term relationships with our clients.

With over 25 years of experience, we offer tailor-made accounting services, 24/7 support, quick service delivery, and excellent customer service. Our goal is to make your life easier by providing essential tools, top-notch support, and customized solutions to meet your accounting needs.

Our Philosophy

Quality

At PCL, we are proud of our reputation for excellence. Our primary goal is to provide services of the highest quality to our clients.

Timeliness

A key to client satisfaction is the ability to meet their statutory and critical deadlines. We strive to complete our engagements in sufficient time, and within the agreed deadlines, to achieve maximum impact and meet the needs of decision makers and other users.

Responsiveness

PCL has effective mechanisms in place to anticipate clients' needs. We strive to respond with innovative, tailored solutions. Keeping communication channels open and listening to our clients is integral to our success.

Value

Finally, PCL emphasizes affordable services. Combined with our commitment to quality, timeliness, and responsiveness, our affordable prices make us a true value in every sense of the word.

More Facts About Us!

PCL was founded in 1999. The Firm provides accounting, tax, and business advisory services to a variety of clients, including Individuals and Corporate entities, including non-profit organizations, and religious institutions.

The President of PCL is a qualified CPA. The Firm and/or its senior staff are members of various professional organizations, including:

  • The American Institute of Certified Public Accountants (AICPA)
  • The National Society of Accountancy (NSA)
  • Chartered Global Management Accountant (CGMA)
  • Greater Washington Society of Certified Public Accountants
  • Virginia Society of Certified Public Accountants
